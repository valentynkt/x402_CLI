## Challenges & Risks

### Technical Challenges

1. **Network Fee Volatility**
   - Ethereum gas spikes make payments uneconomical
   - Solana provides solution but limited networks
   - Cross-chain complexity

2. **Facilitator Centralization**
   - Coinbase controls 80% of transactions
   - Single point of failure risk
   - Antitrust concerns potential

3. **Protocol Standardization**
   - Multiple competing protocols (x402, AP2, TAP)
   - Interoperability challenges
   - Developer confusion

### Regulatory Challenges

1. **KYC/AML for Agents**
   - "Know Your Agent" standards undefined
   - Regulatory uncertainty
   - Compliance burden unclear

2. **Cross-Border Regulations**
   - Different jurisdictions
   - Stablecoin regulations varying
   - Payment processor licensing

3. **Agent Liability**
   - Legal responsibility for agent actions
   - Consumer protection
   - Dispute resolution

### Market Challenges

1. **User Experience**
   - Crypto complexity for mainstream
   - Wallet setup friction
   - Error handling poor

2. **Merchant Adoption**
   - Traditional businesses slow to adopt
   - Integration effort required
   - Uncertain ROI

3. **Competition**
   - Traditional payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
   - Other crypto payment solutions
   - Big tech entering (Google, Visa)
